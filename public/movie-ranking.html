The Best Movies According to Alvin

My Top 3 movies of all-time

Dawn of the Dead

Action movie film about zombie apocalpyse, in which
the survivors needs to face off both zombies and people
to survive.

Legion

The film is about the faith of humankind being tested
by their own desire and greed. The one with the stronger
faith survives.

Constantine

Supernatural film about John Constantine, where he fights
off supernatural beings such as demons to bring balance in
the human realm.
